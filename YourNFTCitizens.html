Why So Much? - Sadly, Animation Ain't Cheap

The average quota per animator on a big budget cartoon is around 20 seconds of completed animation each week! Now imagine how many people are needed to push out a full 24 minutes of an animated show!

Animation costs range from $10,000 to upwards of $50,000 per minute of animation comepleted, or more! Meaning average budgets can range from the hundereds of thousands of dollars, up into the millions!

Our indie projects don't require as extravegant a budget as these top-tier series, but the time and money is still a barrier to entry.

We intentionally chose a simpler style with our charcaters, and a crazy out-there world where nearly anything can happen. One where any character, idea, or environment can find a place to fit in. With that, we're afforded the wiggle room to explore a more complex idea, such as our expanded universe, with a lean, mean, remote animation team.

We want to crowdfund this idea, buy back our artists time from the daily slog, and get their passion and determination focused squarly on making these cartoons for you. And not only that, our expanded universe, WITH you!
